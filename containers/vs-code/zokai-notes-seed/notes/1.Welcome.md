# Welcome to Zokai™ Station

Your AI-powered workspace — notes, research, email, calendar, and AI assistant in one place.

---

## 🚀 First Steps

### 1. Set Up Kilo (Your AI Assistant)
Open the **Kilo panel** on the right sidebar. It starts in **Zokai Guide** mode — your setup assistant.

If you haven't configured your API key yet, follow the [[setup-guide]] to get started.

> **Quick setup**: Enter your OpenRouter key → Save → Click "Start Indexing" → Done.

### 2. Open Your Dashboard
Click the **compass icon** (🧭) in the left Activity Bar → **Dashboard**.
Your emails, calendar, and tasks live here.

### 3. Explore Your Knowledge Graph
Press **`Ctrl+Alt+G`** to visualize connections between your notes.
As you create more notes, the graph grows — revealing patterns you didn't know existed.

## 🤖 Talk to Kilo

Kilo Code is your AI assistant with access to your emails, calendar, notes, and the web.

| Mode | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **Zokai Guide** (default) | Setup help, capability questions, workflow design |
| **Assistant** | Quick tasks, lookups, drafts |
| **Reviewer** | Document analysis, claim validation |
| **Strategist** | Planning, brainstorming, strategy |
| **Tester** | Test execution, QA |
| *Code/Architect/Debug* | Built-in coding modes |

> **Ask the Guide**: Try "What can I do with this station?" for a personalized overview.
